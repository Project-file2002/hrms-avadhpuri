ENTERPRISE WORKFORCE EXPERIENCE PLATFORM (EWXP)
Product Requirements Document (PRD)

1. Product Overview
-------------------
Product name: EWXP — Enterprise Workforce Experience Platform
Former name: HRMS (Human Resource Management System)
Positioning: AI-Powered Enterprise Workforce Experience Platform
Purpose: Build the next-generation workforce platform that goes beyond traditional HRMS record-keeping to deliver intelligent employee experiences, AI-driven automation, and predictive workforce analytics.
Target users: Employees, Managers, HR Business Partners, Recruiters, Payroll Staff, Administrators, CEO/Executives, Candidates.

2. Core Philosophy
-------------------
"Most HRMS platforms only do record management. Our focus is on Employee Experience."

Instead of just storing data, EWXP:
- Understands natural language (HR Copilot)
- Builds a Digital Employee Twin (timeline, projects, salary, performance, skills, learning, attendance, leaves, awards — one screen)
- Provides AI Analytics that proactively tells you attrition risk, promotion due, skill gaps
- Enables No-Code customization (forms, approvals, reports, fields, workflows)
- Creates a Social HR layer (posts, recognition, achievements, announcements, knowledge sharing)
- Supports a Plugin Marketplace to install optional modules without code

3. Goals and Objectives
-----------------------
- Deliver an AI-first workforce experience platform that rivals Workday, Rippling, and Darwinbox
- Replace traditional HRMS with natural language HR Copilot for employees, managers, and HR
- Build a Digital Employee Twin consolidating all employee data in one view
- Provide no-code workflow designer, form builder, and report builder
- Enable predictive analytics: attrition risk, hiring forecast, burnout detection
- Create an organization digital twin with interactive org charts and succession readiness
- Build a unified communication hub with social features
- Support a plugin marketplace for modular expansion
- Ensure enterprise-grade compliance, audit logs, and role-based security

5. User Roles and Permissions
-----------------------------
- Administrator: full access to configuration, plugins, users, all modules, compliance center, custom fields
- HR Manager: employee records, departments, leave/expense approve, performance, recruitment, reports, compliance, career workflows, assets, AI tools
- Manager: employee/department views, leave/expense approve, performance reviews, promotion/transfer workflow steps, recruitment pipeline, reports view
- Employee: own profile, submit own leave/expense, attendance, learning, social HR, AI Copilot, polls, discussions, tasks, meetings
- Payroll Staff: payroll setup, processing, export, reports, approvals, employee list view, attendance view
- Candidate: public career portal only — browse jobs (no login), must login to apply, track application pipeline, AI match/search/explain

7. Functional Requirements
--------------------------

7.1 Employee Management & Digital Employee Twin
- Create, read, update, delete employee records
- Digital Employee Twin: timeline, projects, salary history, performance, skills, learning, attendance, leaves, awards — all on one screen
- Store personal info, employment details, contact, emergency contact, documents
- Employee status: active, probation, resigned, terminated
- Employee Timeline: visual journey from joining date through promotions, transfers, achievements
- Document storage with expiry tracking (visa, passport, contracts)

7.2 Organization Structure
- Interactive Organization Chart with headcount, vacancies, reporting lines, succession readiness
- Manage departments, teams, job roles, grades
- Assign employees to departments and managers
- Organization Digital Twin: real-time view of structure

7.3 Leave Management
- Employee submits leave request
- Workflow: Manager Approval → HR Approval → Payroll Sync → Notification
- Available leave balance calculation
- Leave status tracking: pending, approved, rejected, canceled
- Leave history and entitlement overview

7.4 Attendance
- Daily attendance records with status (Present, Late, Absent, Half-Day)
- Check-in and check-out time tracking
- Manual attendance correction requests (date, corrected times, reason)
- Attendance edit and delete (Admin/HRManager/Manager only — restricted)
- Attendance reports and views

7.5 Performance Management
- Create review cycles and appraisal templates
- 360-degree feedback collection
- Managers rate employee goals, competencies, behavior
- Store comments, ratings, overall score
- Track review status: pending, in progress, completed

7.6 Recruitment & Hiring
- Full workflow: Hiring Request → Approvals → Job Posting → AI Screening → Interview → Offer → Onboarding
- AI Resume Matching and ranking
- AI Interview Question generation
- Candidate status tracking through all pipeline stages (New → Screening → Interviewed → Offered → Hired/Rejected)
- Interview scheduling and feedback
- Offer management and acceptance (default currency: INR)
- Background verification tracking
- Onboarding trigger on joining

7.6a Public Career Portal (Single-Company EWXP)
- Public URL: /careers (no employee login required to browse)
- Job listings: 17+ open requisitions across departments with LPA salary bands
- AI Job Match % — semantic resume vs job description
- Natural language job search (English + Hinglish)
- Explain Job — AI summary of role fit and missing skills
- AI Resume Review — ATS score and suggestions before apply
- **Login required to apply** — Quick Apply redirects unauthenticated users to sign-in/register modal; after auth, user clicks Quick Apply again to open form (no auto-open)
- Quick Apply form: first name, last name, email, phone, resume text; prevents duplicate applications
- On apply: resume text and AI screening summary stored; status auto-set to Screening
- Candidate Register / Login — separate Candidate role JWT (career_token)
- My Applications — candidate-facing tracker with 6-step pipeline synced to HR recruitment module
- Saved jobs and profile text in browser localStorage
- Responsive UI: mobile nav drawer, filter panel (workplace, department, experience, location)
- API: /api/careers/* (config, jobs, match, explain, resume-review, search, assistant, apply, register, login, applications)
- Backend: `[Authorize(Roles = "Candidate")]` enforced on apply endpoint (was `[AllowAnonymous]`)

7.7 Expense Management
- Employee submits expense claims with receipts
- Workflow: Manager Approval → Finance Review → Accounts → Reimbursement
- Expense categories and budgets
- Receipt upload and attachment

7.8 Promotion & Transfer
- Promotion workflow: Manager → HRBP → Department Head → CEO → Payroll Update
- Transfer workflow: Manager → HR → Department → IT → Payroll → Employee
- Salary and grade change tracking
- Effective date management

7.9 Payroll Data Setup
- Define salary components, allowances, deductions
- Map employees to pay structures
- Tax and deduction rules (India-oriented: PF, Professional Tax, TDS in seed data)
- Payroll processing and payslip generation
- Export payroll data
- All monetary amounts displayed in INR (₹) with en-IN locale formatting

7.10 AI HR Copilot
- Natural language HR assistant
- Employee: "Kal leave apply kar do" → Done
- Manager: "Meri team ka attendance dikhao" → Shows
- CEO: "Iss month hiring cost kitni hui?" → Answers
- Policy search: employee asks about company policy, AI answers from stored documents
- Employee Summary, Meeting Summary, Performance Summary
- Salary Insights, Skill Recommendations

7.11 AI Organization Brain
- Instant answers to workforce queries
- "Marketing me kitne log hain?" → AI answers with current count
- Department-wise headcount, attendance summary, leave trends
- No manual searching — natural language input

7.12 Predictive HR Analytics
- System proactively identifies:
  - Attrition Risk (High/Medium/Low) with reasons (salary below market, promotion due, manager rating low)
  - Suggested actions: increase salary, training, manager discussion
- Hiring forecast based on historical data
- Burnout detection from attendance and leave patterns
- Workforce cost prediction

7.17a Digital Workplace Platform (Phase 6 — EWXP Differentiator)
One Employee → One Login → Complete Workplace. Consolidates HRMS + collaboration tools.

Meetings & Calendar:
- Meeting dashboard: today's meetings, upcoming, pending invitations
- Create meeting: title, type (HR/Team/Project/Client/Interview/Training/1:1/Town Hall), participants, department calendar, online link
- AI: agenda generation, conflict detection, duration suggestion, MoM template, post-meeting summary
- Meeting Lifecycle Engine: Create → AI Agenda → Availability → Invitations → Conduct → MoM → Tasks → Reminders

Collaboration Hub:
- Department channels (HR, Engineering, Finance, Sales, Marketing, General)
- Create new channels from UI (name, description, type)
- Messages with full CRUD (post, edit own, delete own or admin)
- Announcements, pinned content within channels
- Responsive split layout (mobile: channel list ↔ chat)

Smart Inbox & Notification Engine:
- Categories: Urgent, Reminder, Information, Task, Approval
- Sources: Attendance, Leave, Payroll, Meeting, Interview, Performance, Documents, AI
- Unified inbox: Today / Yesterday / Earlier, unread, starred
- Future: Email, SMS, WhatsApp, Push, Teams, Slack delivery + user preferences

Announcements:
- Company / Department / Branch scope, priority, acknowledgement required, read tracking

Tasks & Accountability:
- Tasks from meetings with assignee, deadline, completion tracking
- Full CRUD: create, edit, delete, assign, complete with priority levels
- Links to Meeting Lifecycle Engine

Approval Center:
- Single screen for pending Leave, Expense, Promotion, Transfer, Recruitment approvals

Role-Based Workspaces (planned):
- Administrator, HR Manager, Manager, Employee, Payroll Staff — tailored home dashboards

EWXP 5 Pillars:
- People — HR, Recruitment, Employee, Payroll, Performance, Learning
- Work — Meetings, Tasks, Calendar, Approvals, Workflow
- Communicate — Announcements, Hubs, Chat, Notifications
- Intelligence — AI Copilot, Analytics, Meeting AI
- Platform — Admin, Security, Integrations, Audit

7.20 Reporting and Dashboards
- Executive dashboard with KPIs

7.21 Security and Compliance
- Role-based access control with 6 roles (Administrator, HRManager, Manager, Employee, PayrollStaff, Candidate)
- All 34 backend controllers enforce `[Authorize(Roles = "...")]` matching frontend UI gating
- JWT authentication with token expiration (8h for employees, configurable for candidates)
- Rejection reason prompts for all HR approval actions (Leave, Expense, Promotion, Transfer, Offer, Hiring Request)
- Audit logs for all data changes, approvals, and logins
- Input validation and server-side authorization
- Secure AI API key management
- Data encryption at rest and in transit
